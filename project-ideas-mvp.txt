Pitch/Idea:
Create an app that allows users to sign in to input their stock trades to track progress.

MVP:
- Users can sign in to their account
- Users can create posts of their trades
- Users can add photos of stock charts
- Users can caption those photos 
- Users can add statistics such as the time entered, if the trade profitable
	this will allow users to track what they did right and wrong
- Users will be able to access these entries from a feed  to review and study them
- Users will be able to delete and edit posts directly on the feed

Future Features:
-Allow users to follow other users to learn from others
-Have their statistics graphed for visual reference
-Users can leave comments on others posts for feedback
